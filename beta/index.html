How to Install It

1

Tap the download button above

Your browser will download a file called RxOrderly.apk. When it finishes, tap Open in the notification bar, or find it in your Downloads folder.

2

Allow installation from this source

Because this app doesn't come from the Google Play Store, Android will ask for permission to install it. Tap Settings when prompted, then turn on Allow from this source, and go back to complete the installation.

💡 This is normal for beta testing. You can turn this setting off again after installing.
3

Tap Install

Android will show you an installation screen. Tap Install to proceed. The app will be ready in a few seconds.

4

Open RxOrderly

Tap Open after installation, or find the RxOrderly icon on your home screen. The app will walk you through a short setup to get your medications entered.

5

Send feedback

If anything catches your attention — something confusing, something you liked, or something that didn't work — simply reply to Jacques's email. Every message is read personally.
