Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

RxOrderly was built for people who take multiple medications and want to stay organized without the complexity.

01 — Inventory

Supply On Hand

See exactly how many days of supply you have for every medication — sorted by urgency so you always know what needs attention first.

02 — Refills

Request Refills

Contact your prescriber or pharmacy in one tap — by voice, text, or email — with your medication list already prepared.

03 — Research

Drug & Supplement Research

Access FDA label information — interactions, side effects, and warnings — for every medication and supplement you take, without leaving the app.

04 — Savings

Find Savings

Track what you spend on prescriptions, check your eligibility for assistance programs, and discover Medicare and NeedyMeds resources.

05 — Visit Prep

Doctor Visit Prep

Walk into every appointment prepared. Generate a comprehensive PDF packet with your medications, notes, symptoms, and health measurements.

06 — Safety

Medical ID Card

Generate an In Case of Emergency card with your medications, allergies, physicians, and emergency contacts — ready when it matters most.
